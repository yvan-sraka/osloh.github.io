blackbox

a boring new business company from the creators of Cards Against Humanity.

Selling stuff online is tedious and horrible, but at some point we got really good at it. Blackbox lets you sell and ship your stuff for a fraction of the cost and effort of doing it yourself. The shipping is fast. We pay your sales tax. You can customize the packaging and the inserts. It’s pretty great.

We think the future is going to favor independent creators selling their own products directly to fans, without publishers or other bloodsucking middlemen taking most of the money. In fact, we’re betting the company on it.

If you’re making something great, we’d love to make this thing work for you. Blackbox launches next year. If you want updates, sign up here.
